## Publication Format

When adding publications, use this format:

```
**[Year]** | [Authors] | "[Paper Title](DOI/Link)" | *Conference/Journal Name*, Vol. X, pp. XX-XX
```

**Example:**
2024 | Moiz, S., et al. | "[A Novel Approach to Software Testing](https://doi.org/...)" | *IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering*, Vol. 50, pp. 123-135
